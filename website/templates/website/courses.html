Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)

Program Code: BCA

U.G. Program
Duration
3 Years (6 Semesters)
Intake Capacity
60 Seats
Affiliation
State University Approved

Course Overview

Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) is a foundational undergraduate degree focused on computational structures, systems design, software development, database administration, and web engineering. This program equips students with modern programming skills in Java, Python, C++, and database management systems (DBMS), paving the path for roles like Systems Analyst, Software Engineer, or Database Administrator.

Minimum Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must have passed 10+2 (Intermediate) examination in Arts, Science, or Commerce stream with mathematics or computer science as one of the subjects, securing at least 45% marks in aggregate.

Course Overview

Our Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) program trains students in business fundamentals, marketing, financial accounting, human resource operations, and corporate communications. The course includes interactive workshops, company visits, case studies, and business planning models to prepare young minds for corporate leadership positions or entrepreneurship.

Minimum Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must have passed 10+2 (Intermediate) examination in any stream (Arts, Science, or Commerce) from a recognized board, securing at least 45% marks in aggregate.
