Our History & Legacy

Founded in 2011, Patliputra Institute of Technology & Management (PITM) started with a humble batch of 30 computer applications students. Our mission was to bring high-quality technical education to the students of Patna and wider regions of Bihar, bypassing the need for long-distance migration.

Over the past 15 years, PITM has expanded to accommodate more than 1,000 active students on campus across diverse IT and business administrative programs. Affiliated with standard state boards and university guidelines, we emphasize building industry-ready technical capabilities.

With an active alumni base of 2,500+ professionals placed worldwide, PITM continues to lead academic innovation in Patna by introducing specialized tracks, skill-based bootcamps, and industrial certifications.

Welcome to PITM Patna. At our institution, we believe in grooming students to become future leaders who are not just technically sound, but also socially responsible. The IT and business sectors are rapidly evolving, requiring professionals to be agile learners.

We have tailored our campus infrastructure and curriculum guidance to ensure that students receive plenty of practical computer exposure. We encourage academic integrity, group collaboration, and participation in extra-curricular hackathons and sports. Our faculty members work tirelessly to mentor each individual student according to their unique career ambitions.

I welcome you to join our growing academic family and embark on a rewarding journey of personal and professional transformation.

Vision & Mission

The guiding principles behind our educational policies and institutional goals.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a premier center of technical and management education in Eastern India, nurturing innovative professionals who lead with character, knowledge, and technical prowess in the global market.

Our Mission

To deliver high-impact, industry-aligned education, maintain state-of-the-art laboratory assets, recruit and retain outstanding faculty mentors, and provide extensive training and placement support to ensure absolute student success.
